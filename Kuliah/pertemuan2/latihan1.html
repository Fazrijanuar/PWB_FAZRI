Common Foods of the French
You can visit New Orleans and have a great time without ever leaving the picturesque and partyin' French Quarter area, particularly if you pertake of some of these fabulous local foods:
  • Beignets - small deep-fried donuts in powdered sugar. best with a teaming fresh cup of coffe.
  • Seafood Gumbo - a stew-like soup that's delicious. typically served with a side of white rice that's best dumped into the soup directly. skip the chicken gumbo some places serve too: the seafood is definitely better!
  • Jambalaya - the best of all possible dinners. You'll just have to order it so you can find out what it's as necessary for a good time as the bars suggest...
Whatever you do, make sure you have FUN!
